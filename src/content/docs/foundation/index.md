# Foundation Track

The Foundation Track gives every reader a common vocabulary and intuition about AI. These five modules are deliberately tool-rich and math-light. Each can be read on its own as an explainer article.

## Modules

- **F1 — What AI Really Is**: From rules to learning to generation, separating hype from reality, and mapping the AI landscape
- **F2 — Inside GenAI**: How LLMs work, tokens, embeddings, context windows, and the frontier of reasoning
- **F3 — Working with AI**: Prompting fundamentals, advanced patterns, and co-intelligence
- **F4 — From Chatbots to Agents**: Agentic AI, memory, RAG, and multi-agent systems
- **F5 — Responsible AI**: Ethics, bias, fairness, privacy, governance, and regulation
