# AI for Management & Research

This curriculum starts with a **Shared Foundation** — five modules that give every reader a common vocabulary and intuition about AI. After completing the Foundation, choose your track:

- **Foundation Track** — What AI is, how GenAI/LLMs work, prompting, agentic AI, and responsible AI
- **Research Track** — Using AI in the research process and as a research method itself
- **Management Track** — AI-literate management, business applications, and governance
