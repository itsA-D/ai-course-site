# Management Track Capstone

Produce a consulting-grade AI implementation plan for a real organisational problem. This capstone integrates skills from M1 (AI landscape), M2 (prototyping), M3 (data & decisions), M4 (agents), and M6 (governance & change).

## Deliverable

A short deck or memo presenting a complete AI implementation plan, plus an optional working prototype.

## Key requirements

- **Real organisational problem**: Choose a concrete business challenge from your context
- **Data-first thinking**: Diagnose data quality and readiness before model selection
- **Governance built in**: Include responsible-AI checklist and vendor due diligence
- **Change management**: Address resistance, stakeholder plans, and post-deployment monitoring
- **ROI grounded**: Estimate value and cost with realistic assumptions

## Suggested timeline

- Week 1-2: Opportunity diagnosis and data posture assessment
- Week 3-4: Approach selection and prototype development
- Week 5-6: Governance and risk assessment
- Week 7-8: Implementation plan development
- Week 9-10: Final deck or memo with optional prototype demo
